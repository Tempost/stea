﻿Welcome to the NEW South Texas Eventing Association STEA.


We are excited to get the new year started this Sunday, December 4th at the Pine Hill Schooling Horse Trials. This will be our first event of the 2023 show year, so be sure you have joined STEA for your points to count towards some incredible year end prizes. 


https://www.steventing.net/join


Please note that the GHCTA no longer exists. If you would like to compete for year end awards, you and your horse need to belong to STEA. We have included all Life Members (both humans and horses) from GHCTA to STEA. If you were a Life member of GHCTA please check the STEA website to find your membership details. 


We are excited to welcome the first Board members for STEA, and encourage you to reach out to any of them with any questions or suggestions you may have to make this YOUR local eventing organization.
